French Vanilla Cake

Try this super moist and delicious french vanilla cake recipe! It is simple to make, and it turns out perfect every time!

Ingredients Recipe Nutrition

Ingredients

  1. Betty Crocker French Vanilla cake mix
  2. 1 Cup of milk
  3. 1/2 Cup of vegetable oil
  4. 3 Eggs

Recipe

  1. Heat the oven to 350ยบ F degrees.
  2. Add cake mix, milk, eggs, and oil to a bowl and beat vigorously with a spatula for 2 minutes.
  3. Grease the bottom only of a pan.
  4. Pour the mix into the pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from the pan and cool completely before frosting.
